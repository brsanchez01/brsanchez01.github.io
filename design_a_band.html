Challenge: Design a Band

Challenge Information

Design thinking is when you try solving a problem by using empathy towards the people. The reason of why we are studying this is because when you are making a product you have to know how people feel about it, you have to get their ideas in order for your product to be successful. We also study this because you have to know what the people feel.

In this challenge, we used the DEEP design process to design a band. The first step of the DEEP process is discover. In the discover step we got to see the many things that people liked. In the empathize step we tried to feel what our peers thought or felt by asking them questions on what types of fonts they liked what colors they liked. In the Experiment step we put all the ideas and thoughts from the peers that we asked questions from and we tried to make something out of it or atleast get ideas from them to make our own product. In the Produce step we are done with everything and we see our finished product of what we got from the ideas of our peers. A major piece of the challenge was to create an album cover for the band. Band Biography

On July 1, 2010 three best friends of hispanic origin, by the names of Josiah, Jeremiah, and Joshua were on their way to become senior dropouts from a high school in South Los Angeles called John C. Fremont High School, Not more than two weeks later all three were expelled because they hosted an illegal concert of electronic music. They responded back by saying that it was a protest to the unfair and unjust government and high school laws and rules, but they were ignored. After a year had passed the three friends moved to San Jose, there they decided that they would keep on trying to protest against unfair laws and rules with their music, and so they decided to start a band.

Since Josiah's family was economically well, due to his dad owning half of the liquor stores in Los Angeles, he decided to supply the materials needed for the band.
